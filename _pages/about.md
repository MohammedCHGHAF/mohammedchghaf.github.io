I am a Localization & HD Mapping Engineer specializing in Automated Driving and Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (AD/ADAS). With a strong background in computer vision, robotics, and SLAM techniques, I am passionate about developing cutting-edge solutions for automated navigation.

My expertise
======
- **Localization & HD Mapping**: Precision positioning for automated vehicles.

- **SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)**: Multimodal approaches for real-time environment perception.

- **Computer Vision & AI**: Leveraging visual descriptors for pose estimation and odometry.

- **Embedded Systems & Data Processing**: Optimizing performance for real-world applications.

- **Research & Development**: Bridging the gap between academic innovations and industrial applications.
